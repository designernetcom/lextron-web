Transducerized Tightening System

A Transducerized Tightening System is an advanced tool used in manufacturing to tighten bolts or screws with very high accuracy and control. It uses smart sensors (transducers) to monitor and control tightening in real-time.

  • ✔ Torque Control (force applied)
  • ✔ Angle Control (rotation precision)
  • ✔ Seating Point Detection
  • ✔ Torque Slope Monitoring

Ensures every bolt is tightened perfectly — improving consistency and product quality.

Power Range: 0.3 Nm to 500 Nm (from small devices to heavy machinery)
